ABOUT US


Amara Global Recruitment is an elite EPC Recruitment Company, head quartered in Bangalore with a branch office in Mumbai and associate offices in more than 15 Cities across India. We have an immense number of clientele spanning in the Middle East, APAC and the US. We attend to our clients from our vast databases, online portals and various agencies. We’ve structured our operations into various specialised Industry groups. Each group is headed by a Recruitment Manager who is minutely screened and selected from the respective industry and chosen for his/her expertise and relevant experiences.

Our recruiters are qualified with valid years of experience and are specialized in various types of placement processes. Our team comprises of Senior Technical Consultants & HR Executives, who screen and select the best candidates according to our clients’ requirements. We have a professional network of HR co-ordinators, subordinate staff, IT team, Visa, Emigration & ticketing staff, admin & accounts staff, to run our recruitment business more speedily and smoothly. We ensure our clients a hassle-free recruitment assistance and procedure.
